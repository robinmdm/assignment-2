AFTER FINISHING, WHAT'S NEXT?

After finishing Cyber Security you have to think about what direction you want to dive into first. You can try your luck as Pentester, who test software, devices and services for their security, creating reports after conducting the security tests and letting the customer know what they can improve upon.", "Many modern software development companies involve cyber security roles in the develepment process. Here you are either responsible for the secure architecture, secure devops processes or secure coding.

Maybe you don't like coding and want to do incident response, which gives you the task to supervise networks and systems, reacting appropriate if an attack is detected. You will have to be able to respond quickly to any malicious act, knowing the steps involved to reduce the damage, stop the attack and report the incident in detail.
