Pathfinder is an online educational platform that helps students find the most suitable course for their respective study programs all over the world. It allows students to search, compare, and apply for a course in just 10 minutes. The portal also provides dedicated e-mail customer support within 24 hours. We offer a service to connect students to universities by giving them a centralized collection of informatioon.

No longer will you need to waste hours and days browsing through google trying to find the perfect course, just select your country / location, your preferences and we will show you all the options. You will even get direct links to the application process you won't waste time trying to make sense of the convoluted unviversity website interfaces! Furthermore, our guradians will love to help you with any questions you have regarding a field or course and give you a better idea what could suit you most.
