WHY CYBER SECURITY?

Cyber Security has raising job opportunities in the future and will get your career started in an ever-growing field! It is the perfect field for people who like to solve problems and want to be live-long learners. You will be able to do a good deed by using your cyber security knowledge to protect companies and people from malicious actors. There is a lot of different specializations making it an interesting choice with many options down the road (e.g. Incident Response, Cyber Security Engineer, Security Architect, etc...)
